## Brand Authority Feature Overview

### Setting Up Brand Authority (Complete Beginner's Guide)

The Brand Authority feature is simple: **Upload 3 images of yourself**, and every time the AI post generator creates posts with images, it will automatically place your images in each generated image. This creates the ultimate brand identity and authority - people will recognize your posts instantly!

**What It Does:**
- You upload 3 personal images (photos of yourself)
- AI automatically places these images in every generated post image
- Maintains visual consistency automatically
- Builds brand recognition over time
- Creates ultimate brand identity and authority

> üí° **Key Point:** This is specifically about your personal images being automatically placed in AI-generated post images. Every time you generate a post with an image, your 3 uploaded images will be integrated into that image automatically!

**Let's Set It Up Together:**

#### Step 1: Access Brand Authority Settings

1. **Log into your platform**
2. **Click "Settings"** (usually in the top menu or sidebar)
3. **Look for "Brand Authority"** or "Personal Branding"
4. **Click it**

**What you'll see:** A page with options to upload images and configure settings

#### Step 2: Prepare Your 3 Personal Images (This is Important!)

<div align="center">

### üì∏ You Need 3 Images of Yourself

**These will be automatically placed in every AI-generated post image!**

---

**Image 1: Your Primary Photo**
- **What:** Your professional headshot or clear photo of yourself
- **Why:** This is what people will recognize most
- **Example:** A clear photo of you smiling, professional background
- **Best Practice:** High-quality, well-lit, professional appearance

**Image 2: Secondary Photo**
- **What:** You in a different pose, angle, or setting
- **Why:** Adds variety while maintaining consistency
- **Example:** You working, speaking, in action, or different angle
- **Best Practice:** Different from Image 1 but still professional

**Image 3: Tertiary Photo**
- **What:** Another photo of yourself (different from 1 and 2)
- **Why:** Adds another layer of brand recognition
- **Example:** You in a different setting, pose, or context
- **Best Practice:** Maintains professional quality, different from first two

#### Step 3: Upload Your Images

**Image Requirements (Don't Skip This!):**

**Size:**
- **Minimum:** 1080x1080 pixels
- **Better:** 2000x2000 pixels or higher
- **Why:** Higher quality = better results

**Format:**
- **JPG** or **PNG** files
- **Why:** These work best with the system

**File Size:**
- **Under 5MB per image**
- **Why:** Too large = slow upload

**Quality:**
- **High resolution** (clear, not blurry)
- **Professional lighting** (not too dark or bright)
- **Clean background** (or transparent for logos)
- **Consistent style** (all 3 images should match in style)

**How to Upload:**

1. **Click "Upload Image 1"** or "Choose File"
2. **Select your first image** from your computer
3. **Wait for upload** (may take 10-30 seconds)
4. **Repeat for Image 2 and Image 3**
5. **Wait for processing** (system analyzes your images)

**Common Beginner Mistakes:**
- ‚ùå Using low-quality phone photos (use high-res!)
- ‚ùå Inconsistent lighting across images
- ‚ùå Cluttered backgrounds (keep it clean!)
- ‚ùå Images that don't match your brand style
- ‚ùå Forgetting to wait for processing

**Frequency Settings (How Often Your Images Appear in AI-Generated Post Images):**

**Option 1: Every Post (Maximum Recognition)**
- Your images appear in every AI-generated post image
- **Best for:** Building recognition quickly
- **Use when:** You're new and want maximum visibility
- **Example:** Every AI-generated post image includes your images

**Option 2: Every Other Post (Balanced)**
- Your images appear in 50% of AI-generated post images
- **Best for:** Most people (recommended!)
- **Use when:** You want recognition without being too obvious
- **Example:** Post 1 has your images, Post 2 doesn't, Post 3 has them, etc.

**Option 3: Every Third Post (Subtle)**
- Your images appear in 33% of AI-generated post images
- **Best for:** Established brands
- **Use when:** You want subtle branding
- **Example:** Every 3rd AI-generated post image includes your images
